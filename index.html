VAIKARYA’S APPROACH- INVESTING BEHIND CHANGES

• VaiKArya's portfolio approach is to create a concentrated best-idea portfolio of the most attractive CHANGES across the market cap and sectors. During their first cycle of CHANGES, stocks are in special situations, while later (2nd, 3rd) cycles of CHANGES make stocks quality compounders. We focus on out-of-the-radar or under-discovered stocks with a long growth runway.   • We hunt for CHANGES that have high odds of an orbit shift in growth, ROIC, or shareholder-friendly actions from the companies. These CHANGES include management changes, direct beneficiary or 1st/2nd derivatives of Megatrends, industry cycle shifts, regulatory/policy cycles, corporate actions, etc.   We need only a couple of fresh ideas in a year. Finding these amongst ~1,000 companies with >$100 mn float is reasonably uncorrelated to the market cycles.
